# 🌸 Work-Life Balance as a Mom with Hobbies  

## 💡 Finding the Balance  

Being a **developer, a mom, and having hobbies** is a constant juggling act.

---

## 👩‍💻 Work as a Developer  

Working in tech means **constant learning and problem-solving**, but I’ve developed strategies to manage my workload while still making time for family:  

✅ **Time Blocking** – I set dedicated work hours and stick to them.  
✅ **Prioritization** – Breaking tasks into high/low priority helps me stay focused.  

---

## 🎨 Hobbies: My Me-Time  

Some things I love:  

🎨 **Creative Outlets** – Painting, crochet and drawing with my daughter.  
📚 **Music** – Listening to music and playing the drums.  
🏃‍♀️ **Exercise** – Gym and hiking.   
😁 **Socializing** – Spending time with family and friends.   

💡 **How I Make Time for Hobbies:**  
✔ **Early Mornings or Late Nights** – Peaceful moments for creativity.  
✔ **Combining Activities** – Listening to music while doing chores.
